Titan Raga Cocktails

Titan has always believed that the Raga woman is elegant, graceful and undeniably charming. Reflecting the beauty of her soul is a shine that is unmistakably unique and bright. A shine, that comes from within and is truly mesmerising. Inspired by this unmatched and innate sparkle is the Raga Cocktails range. A series of Swarovski encrusted watches that echo the elegance of the contemporary Raga woman. With their brilliant mother-of-pearl dials, magnetic clasps and a beautiful cocktail ring to match, each timepiece is a glorious testament to unparalleled beauty.

Celebrating 150 Years Of A Timeless Legacy

Titan Watches salutes the prodigious efforts of Jamsetji Tata, founder of the Tata group, with this curated collection of watches.These elegant and minimalist watches bring to life the clarity of thought and the foresight with which Jamsetji chiselled a direction for the Tata Group. These watches are available for both men and women and come in both leather and steel strap options. Marking 150 years is just another milestone in a legacy that is timeless.

Sonata...What makes us different

With the widest assortment of contemporary and timeless designs, Sonata, India's largest selling watch brand is etched in the hearts of every Indian. With an eye for aesthetics and through relentless innovation and sheer passion, we have created more than 700 watch designs that are available across 8,000+ authorized watch outlets and the exclusive World of Titan stores.

Titan EyePlus

Titan EyePlus, the third major venture of consumer business by Titan Company, was launched into the organized eyewear segment in March 2007.

Fastrack

Fastrack was launched in 1998 and became an independent urban youth brand in 2005. Since then, it has carved a niche for itself with watches and sunglasses.

Tanishq

Tanishq has emerged as fastest growing jewellery brand and is a name which signifies superior craftsmanship, exclusive designs.
